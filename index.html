📤 Share Website Ga Abokai Sau Ɗaya

1. Koyi Skill Daya Yadda Ya Kamata

Kar ka bi kowane trend. Zabi coding, graphics, video editing, ko writing. Koyi shi har ƙarshe.

2. Fara Da Abin Da Kake Da Shi

Ba dole sai laptop mai ₦500k ba. Wayarka ta isa ka fara. YouTube + Google su ne malamanka.

3. Gina Network, Ba Kudi Kawai Ba

Abokanka na ADUSTECH su ne dukiyarka. Taimaka musu, koyar da su. Network > Money.

4. Kada Ka Tsaya Wajen Koro

Mutane za su ce "ba za ka iya ba". Ka nuna musu da aiki, ba da magana ba.

5. Koyi Turanci Kadai

Turanci kofa ce ta duniya. Kada ka ji kunya. Koyi kowace rana kalma 5.

6. Ajiye Ƙaramin Kudi

Ko ₦50 kowace rana. Bayan wata 1 za ka sami ₦1500. Ƙarami yana tara ya zama babba.

7. Koyi Yadda Ake Siyarwa

Ko kana da skill, sai ka iya sayar da ita. Koyi magana, rubutu, yadda ake gamsar da mutane.

8. Zama Mai Neman Ilimi

Kada ka ce "na gama karatu". Duniya tana canzawa kowace rana. Koyi sabo koyaushe.

9. Yi Aiki Ba Ko Ta Yaya Ba

Idan kana jiran "yanayi ya daidaita" ba za ka fara ba. Fara da rashin kayan aiki.

10. Ka Yi Amana Da Allah

Ka yi aiki, ka yi addu'a. Sauran Allah zai kula. Haƙuri + Aiki = Nasara.

1. Master One Skill First

Don't chase every trend. Pick coding, graphics, video editing, or writing. Master it completely.

2. Start With What You Have

You don't need a ₦500k laptop. Your phone is enough to start. YouTube + Google are your teachers.

3. Build Network, Not Just Money

Your ADUSTECH friends are your wealth. Help them, teach them. Network > Money.

4. Don't Stop at Criticism

People will say "you can't". Show them with work, not words.

5. Learn English Only

English is the key to the world. Don't be shy. Learn 5 words every day.

6. Save Small Money

Even ₦50 daily. After 1 month you'll have ₦1500. Small becomes big.

7. Learn Sales

Even with skill, you must sell it. Learn talking, writing, how to convince people.

8. Be a Lifelong Learner

Don't say "I'm done learning". The world changes daily. Learn something new always.

9. Take Action Anyway

If you wait for "perfect conditions" you won't start. Start with no tools.

10. Trust Allah

Work hard, make dua. The rest Allah will handle. Patience + Work = Success.
